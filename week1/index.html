A few lines about who I am

I am a young man from Neot Golan originally, I was born in Damascus - Syria, Until before I arrived to Athens two years ago, I studied at the Faculty of Law at the University of Damascus, Adaptation in Greece was not easy as a refugee, I did not know a language, I did not know where to start, and where I’m going! When I left my homeland the planning was different, the goal was still the same - to seek a more peaceful life, a promising future and Career - but things changed a little on the way and found myself here with only two options :
1. Adapt and beat with all you might to reach the goal.
2. To blame the circumstances and fate and to be idle for a few months until finally I return to the point from which I left.
The second option was not an option for me at all, it's not me and it's not my personality, For two years all I could do to advance myself and stand on my feet I did, Until one day I saw an ad from Social Hackers Academy And at that moment I knew it was going to be my future: Here I will learn, succeed, do, and break through a successful and productive career.
